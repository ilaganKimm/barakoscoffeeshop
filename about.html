About Us

Welcome to Barakos Coffee Shop, where passion for coffee meets unparalleled craftsmanship. Since our establishment in 2022, we have been dedicated to delivering an extraordinary coffee experience to our valued customers.

Nestled in the heart of Batangas, our cozy and inviting space is a haven for coffee lovers, where they can indulge in the perfect cup of coffee and immerse themselves in the rich flavors and aromas that define our craft. We are proud to offer an extensive selection of coffee beans and ground coffee, allowing you to enjoy the exceptional taste of our offerings wherever you are.

At Barakos Coffee Shop, we believe that coffee is an art form. That's why we meticulously source only the finest coffee beans from around the country. From the verdant plantations of the south to the lush hillsides of the north, our beans are carefully selected for their unique characteristics and exceptional quality.

We collaborate directly with farmers and growers who share our commitment to sustainability, fair trade, and ethical practices, ensuring that every cup of coffee we serve leaves a positive impact on the communities and ecosystems it originates from. In addition to our freshly brewed coffee, we also provide a wide range of ground coffee options, allowing you to savor the flavors of our carefully selected beans in the comfort of your own home.

Our team of skilled baristas and coffee enthusiasts brings their expertise and passion to every brew. With meticulous attention to detail, we roast our coffee beans in-house, embracing the delicate balance between time, temperature, and technique to unlock the full potential of each bean's flavor profile.

Whether you prefer the smooth and velvety texture of a perfectly crafted latte, the bold and robust notes of an espresso shot, or the delicate nuances of a hand-poured pour-over, our baristas are dedicated to creating a personalized and memorable coffee experience tailored to your preferences. Our ground coffee is skillfully prepared to preserve the flavor and aroma, ensuring that every cup you brew at home captures the essence of our exceptional coffee.

Beyond exceptional coffee, Barakos Coffee Shop is a place where connections are forged, friendships are made, and conversations flow as freely as the aroma of freshly brewed coffee. We pride ourselves on creating a warm and welcoming environment, where our community can come together and share in the love of coffee.

From the first sip to the last, we invite you to embark on a sensory journey, guided by our passion for coffee, dedication to quality, and commitment to providing an exceptional experience that will leave you craving for more. Explore our selection of coffee beans and ground coffee, and let us accompany you on your coffee adventure, whether you choose to enjoy it in our welcoming space or in the comfort of your own home.
